# Alignment Evaluator

A place to evaluate and place anything/anyone based loosely on the D&D Alignment framework. Useful for tough judgment calls. 

How the Axis are defined:

The X-Axis (Lawful - Chaotic)

The lawful and chaotic variables are defined by the phsychological concepts of the "id" and "superego". The "id" having impulsive and
primal characteristics, the "superego" representing discipline and self-control and the "ego" being the mediator between. 

chaotic = (id > superego)  
lawful = (id < superego)  
neutral = (id === superego)   

The Y-Axis (Evil - Good)

Good and evil in this case are determined by impact on others. Altruism and looking out for others is generally considered "good" and is a useful concept for this case. Self-interest at the expense of others is considered "evil". 
Simply put,

good = (benefit > harm)  
evil = (benefit < harm)  
neutral = (benefit === harm)
